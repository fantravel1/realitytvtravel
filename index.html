Common

Set-jetting is traveling to destinations featured in movies and TV shows. The phenomenon has grown into a $66 billion industry, with 61% of travelers now influenced by screen content when choosing destinations.

Many reality TV locations are publicly accessible or available to rent. Resorts, restaurants, and some villas can be booked. Private mansions like the Bachelor Mansion occasionally appear on Airbnb.

The Bachelor Mansion (Villa de la Vina) rents for $6,000 to $30,000 per night on Airbnb, depending on dates and event type. The property hosts weddings, proposals, and special events.

Yes! Love Is Blind films at publicly bookable all-inclusive resorts. Grand Velas Riviera Maya starts around $800-1,500/night, and TRS Hotels range from $400-1,000/night.
