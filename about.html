What is Set-Jetting?

Set-jetting is the growing travel trend of visiting destinations featured in movies and TV shows. According to recent studies, this phenomenon has grown into a $66 billion industry, with 61% of travelers now influenced by screen content when choosing their vacation destinations.

For reality TV fans, this means you can walk the same grounds as your favorite Bachelor contestants, stay in the actual villas from Too Hot to Handle, or visit the resorts where Love Is Blind couples said "I do."
